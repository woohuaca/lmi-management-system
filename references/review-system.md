# Review System

Use this reference when the user asks for any level of review or retrospective.

## Review Layers

The skill should support four review layers:

- `Daily Review`: correct execution and attention
- `Weekly Review`: correct rhythm, leverage, and weekly focus
- `Monthly Review`: correct goals, mechanism, and resource allocation
- `Role Review`: correct role definition, responsibility focus, and time investment

## Daily Review

Focus on:

- today's most important result
- completion versus intention
- interruptions and attention drift
- what should be protected tomorrow
- what should be delegated, delayed, or dropped

## Weekly Review

Focus on:

- weekly top goals and actual results
- high-return activities versus low-return time
- root cause split:
  - target issue
  - execution issue
  - mechanism issue
- what to continue, stop, delegate, and carry forward

## Monthly Review

Focus on:

- this month's personal focus goals
- this month's company focus goals
- whether high-return activities truly produced results
- whether management mechanisms are working
- what should be kept, removed, strengthened, or redesigned next month

## Role Review

Focus on:

- whether the role still reflects the real value expected
- whether top responsibilities match real time investment
- whether key results are still the right ones
- whether high-return activities truly belong to this role
- whether the role needs sharper boundaries, delegation, or mechanism support

## Review Output Rule

Every review should end with:

- clear lessons
- a small number of adjustments
- `Next 1-3 Moves`
