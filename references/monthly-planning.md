# Monthly Planning

Use this reference when turning role goals or annual goals into a monthly plan.

## Monthly Planning Rules

- Start with mission or purpose, then move into monthly focus.
- Distinguish personal focus goals from company focus goals.
- Keep personal focus goals grouped by life domains when relevant.
- Capture top personal and company goals in one visible priority area.
- Use the urgent and important planning tables to translate goals into action.
- Reserve a section for high-return activities that support those goals.

## Common Monthly Sections

- Mission or purpose
- This month's personal focus goals
- This month's company focus goals
- Top personal and company goals
- High-return activity items
- Urgent items plan
- Important items plan
