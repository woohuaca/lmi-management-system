# Daily Planning

Use this reference for daily work planning and daily review.

## Daily Planning Rules

- Start from weekly outcomes and monthly priorities.
- Start by importing yesterday's daily review and unfinished items before adding new work.
- Re-check each unfinished item from yesterday and decide whether it should continue today, move later this week, be delegated, or be dropped.
- Use the LMI categories from the original form:
  - `A` important items
  - `B` urgent items
  - `C` contact or follow-up items
  - `D` meetings, discussions, or coordination items
- Separate important items, urgent items, contact or follow-up items, meetings or discussions, schedule, and completed items.
- Put appointments and time-specific work on the schedule side.
- Keep the left side for priority decisions, not just raw capture.
- Mark progress status when relevant: delegated, in progress, postponed, completed.

## Daily Review Rules

- What result moved today?
- What interrupted focus?
- What should be protected tomorrow?
- What should be delegated, declined, or delayed?
- Which unfinished items should be carried into tomorrow's draft, and under which category?
